The Port MacKenzie Special Use District provides
commercial, industrial, and waterfront dependent
ground lease sites to support maritime facilities
and operations. The Borough offers long or short
term, competitive lease rates for sites varying
from as small as two acres up to hundreds of
acres if needed. Exclusive operational rights can
be negotiated as well as economic incentives to
attract job-creating industries.
