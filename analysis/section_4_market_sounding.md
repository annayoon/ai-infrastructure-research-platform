# Section 4. Korean Market Sounding Strategy & Target Identification

## 4.1 Market Sounding Objectives

The primary objectives of the market sounding effort are to:
- Validate commercial AI infrastructure demand
- Identify qualified APAC and U.S. customer prospects
- Evaluate compute, power, cooling, and connectivity requirements
- Assess long-term infrastructure scalability requirements
- Identify potential anchor tenants and strategic infrastructure partners

The market sounding effort is intended to support:
- Customer acquisition strategy
- Infrastructure positioning validation
- Government engagement
- Strategic partnership development
- Potential compute offtake opportunities

---

South Korea represents a strategically important market due to:
- Rapid AI adoption

- Increasing AI cloud infrastructure requirements

Potential Korean customer categories include:
- Cloud providers
- Semiconductor ecosystem companies
- AI platform companies
- Enterprise AI adopters
- Strategic AI initiatives

Key Korean infrastructure considerations include:
- Long-term compute scalability
- AI training infrastructure
- GPU cloud capacity
