# Strategic Roadmap Summary

The project roadmap is structured around the convergence of:
- AI infrastructure growth
- Dispatchable power requirements
- Fiber resiliency and diversification
- Cooling efficiency advantages
- National security priorities
- Arctic strategic positioning

The phased roadmap supports the gradual transition from strategic validation to infrastructure execution readiness.
