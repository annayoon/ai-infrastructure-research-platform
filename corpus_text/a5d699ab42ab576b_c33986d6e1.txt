Accepted 16 August 2024
                                                       major challenge to the industry’s net-zero goal. To address this, China has launched a bold initiative to
                                                       relocate data centers to the western region, leveraging natural cooling, clean energy, and cost-effective
                                                       resources. By 2030, this move is expected to reduce emissions from the data center sector by 16%–
                                                       20%, generating direct economic benefits of approximately 53 billion USD. The success of this initiative
